About Me

I’m a PhD researcher at Imperial College London, co-supervised by IBM Research, where I develop and apply novel machine learning and optimal transport methods across a range of domains. My work focuses on connecting rigorous research with practical impact through collaborations, international projects, and industrial partnerships.

As I approach the final year of my PhD, I’m looking for research scientist roles that will allow me to broaden my experience across computer science and machine learning, and to continue building methods that translate effectively between theory and application.
